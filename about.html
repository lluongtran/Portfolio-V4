About Me



Introduction
Hi, I am Lamont. I am a UX Developer & technology enthusiast. I studied UX/UI from UC Irvine. I also studied Economics & Accounting from UC San Diego. I worked over the last 6 years in the technology sector; specializing in finance, business & IT development, and CRM design/integration. I also worked with programming languages such as PHP, HTML, Javascript, and SQL. I love researching emerging technologies such as IoT, AI, and blockchain and finding ways to integrate them into UX.


Design Philosphy
• I believe a successful design is one that maximazies team collaboration
• I believe a design should start from the results found in the the UX research
• I believe usability testing is critical for all designs
• I believe a strong understanding of the underlying technology allows us to effectively deliver a solution
• I believe a design needs to align with the business model and take into account scalability and feasability
